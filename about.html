About me

My name is Mark Allingham, I am a 23 year old graduate in Engineering from the University of Cambridge from Northern Ireland. The first two years of my degree focussed on a wide range of engineering disciplines, including Mechanical, Structural, Electrical and Information Engineering. In my third and fourth years, I took modules in Aerospace, Environmental and Energy Engineering. I am also a keen linguist; as part of my degree in third year, I undertook an engineering project conducted and assessed in French and have since continued my studies in my spare time and commenced studies in Spanish and Russian. Outside of academics, I enjoyed a variety of sports including Rowing, Rugby and Cycling.

About this site

Whilst at university, I developed a keen insterest in coding and a variety of programming languages. During lockdown I wanted to use any spare time I had developing new and existing skills. So, I started this website as a project to help me expand my programming knowledge and to introduce me to new programming languages. I keep this site active to act as a source of motivation for working on technical projects which in turn allows me to continue learning and developing as an engineer. The vast majority of this website's code was written by me - with the exception of some content used to set the theme, which was inspired from the resources at Bootswatch.

This website is hosted by Netlify and deploys automatically from a GitHub repository.
