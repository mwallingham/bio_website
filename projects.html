Packed Bed Thermal Reservoirs

University of Cambridge: Part IIB Project

Packed bed thermal reservoirs are a technology used for high-density energy storage. The major components of these devices are the containment vessel, solid packing medium and the working fluid. Typical implementations for this technology use low density working fluids such as argon gas - this research was interested in using high denisty fluids like water as the working fluid. The objective of the project was to create a testing rig from which to gather experimental data in order to validate and identify potential improvements for an existing FORTRAN model.
